Our Story

Since 2019

A Mediterranean Heart in the Heart of Prague

Ember & Oak was born from a simple belief: that fire transforms food into something extraordinary. Founded in 2019 by Chef Marco Bellini, our restaurant brings the warmth of Mediterranean tradition to the cobblestoned streets of Prague's Old Town.

Every evening, our wood-fired ovens and grills come alive, filling the space with the primal aromas of charring herbs, caramelizing sugars, and slow-roasting meats. This is cooking as it was meant to be — elemental, honest, alive.

The Chef

Marco Bellini

Born in Naples and trained across the kitchens of Barcelona, Lyon, and Copenhagen, Chef Marco Bellini brings over two decades of culinary mastery to every plate. His philosophy is rooted in respect — for fire, for the seasons, and for the artisans who grow and raise our ingredients.

At Ember & Oak, Marco has created a menu that honors Mediterranean heritage while embracing the extraordinary produce of the Czech countryside. The result is food that feels both timeless and entirely new.

Our Philosophy

What We Believe

Seasonal

Our menu evolves with the seasons, celebrating each ingredient at its peak. We work with local farmers to bring you the freshest produce each week.

Local

We source from farms within 100 kilometers of Prague. Every partnership is personal, every ingredient traceable to the hands that grew it.

Crafted

From hand-rolled pasta to house-cured charcuterie, everything is made with patience and precision. There are no shortcuts in our kitchen.
